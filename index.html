High Conviction Investing in Immigrant Founders from Day 1

Capital

We engage with founders at idea stage, often being the first investor to commit capital, ranging from $150k to $250k, and supporting them to close their funding round.

Immigration Support

We'll back founders regardless of their visa and settlement situation. We provide immigration support and can sponsor visas, to help founders transition into entrepreneurship.

Network Access

We give the founders we back access to downstream VC funding and we'll introduce them to our existing network of clients and partners.
